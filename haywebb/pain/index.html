Proximity sensor : A device that detects the presence or absence of a nearby object.

Water quality sensor : These sensors play an important role as they monitor the quality of water for different purposes.

IR sensors : An infrared sensor is a sensor which is used to sense certain characteristics of its surroundings by either emitting or detecting infrared radiation.
